## Laura Becker – Web Designer

### Background: ###

Laura is an experienced web designer with over 6 years of professional experience in designing and implementing modern,
responsive websites. She combines technical expertise in **HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript** with a keen sense
for **UX design, accessibility and visual brand communication**. Through her work on complex projects – from
e-commerce relaunches to corporate websites and e-learning platforms – she has developed a deep understanding of
user-centred design and performance optimisation.

### Goals: ###

- Design user experiences that combine aesthetics and functionality
- Establish accessible and SEO-optimised designs as standard
- Efficiently documenting design systems and making them scalable
- Promoting sustainable brand communication through consistent digital designs

### Behavioural traits: ###

- Analytical in UX research and target group-oriented in the design process
- High quality standards for responsiveness, usability and loading times
- Close collaboration with development and marketing teams
- Open to feedback and continuous improvement through A/B testing and user feedback
- Enthusiastic about design tools such as **Figma**, **Adobe XD** and working with **design systems**
